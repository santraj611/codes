### Article Reading
#article #book 
Things to help you when reading some article or some book

1. **Break it down**: Read one sentence at a time and try to summarize it in your own words.
2. **Look up complex words**: Use tools like dictionaries or apps to check meanings and synonyms for better understanding.
3. **Active reading**: Highlight important ideas or make notes as you read. This will help with retention.
4. **Practice re-reading**: Don’t hesitate to read the paragraph again; this often brings clarity.
